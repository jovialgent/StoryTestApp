Naming conventions for all blog posts will be: 

blog_name~Of~Post

Special Markers 

[Story] [/Story] - Story part that will be broken up to parts. 
[Title] [/Title] - Title of the blog post.
[Data] [/Date] - Date of the blog post.
[Subtitle] [/Subtitle] - The subtitle of the story.
[Synopsis] [/Synopsis] - Synopsis of the story
[Paragraphs] [/Paragraphs] - Paragraph parts
[Tags]tag1,tag2,tag3[/Tags] - Tags that are associate with this post
[Parasynopsis] [/Parasynopsis] - This marker will sum up each paragraph for mining
